opening the app for the first time

because six impossible things is an independent app exclusive to subscribers, your computer will show a warning the first time you open it. this is normal and only happens once at install.

on windows: click More info, then Run anyway.

on mac: once you add the app to your Applications folder and click to open it,
macOS will show a warning message. click Done or Cancel (do not move it to the trash). then go to System Settings → Privacy & Security and scroll down to the Security section. next to "Six Impossible Things was blocked" click Open Anyway. confirm once more in the pop-up window, and it will open normally from then on.

six impossible things is full of art + color so it may take a minute to open the first time. after that it should open quickly each day. hope you enjoy it.
